GStreamer is a Free & Open Source cross-platform multimedia framework.

It allows you to quickly develop powerful media-centric applications and to take advantage of the latest advances in codec & filter technology. If something is missing, you can add new codecs and filters by writing simple plugins with a clean, standardized interface.
