Online

GStreamer is made by many contributors around the world. Developers typically interact on the #gstreamer IRC channel on Freenode, and on the following mailing lists:

  • gstreamer-devel
    Where general discussions occur. Ask here if you have problems with GStreamer.
    Please submit patches and report bugs in bugzilla and not on this list.

  • gstreamer-openmax
    Development of GStreamer wrappers for OpenMax components.
  • gstreamer-android
    Development of GStreamer for Android integration.
  • gstreamer-embedded
    Discussion of building, optimising, developing and using GStreamer on embedded devices.

In person

There is an annual conference, and usually a hackfest or two, in various cities around the world.

  • The GStreamer Conference is an event for developers, decision makers and anyone else interested in GStreamer and open source multimedia. THe 2013 edition is now over. See 2014 for planning. Previous conferences: 2013, 2012, 2011.
  • The GStreamer Hackfest is a smaller event where GStreamer developers gather to tackle "big picture" technical challenges or work on specific problems that interest them. Any software developer who wants to get involved deeper and contribute to GStreamer is welcome to join.
